YouTube Download Troubleshooting Guide

❓ Video Not Downloading?

If your YouTube video isn't downloading, try these solutions:

  • • Check if the video URL is correct and accessible
  • • Ensure the video is public and not age-restricted
  • • Try a different video quality option
  • • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • • Disable browser extensions temporarily

🐌 Slow Download Speed?

To improve download speeds:

  • • Choose a lower quality for faster downloads
  • • Check your internet connection speed
  • • Close other bandwidth-intensive applications
  • • Try downloading during off-peak hours
  • • Use a wired connection instead of Wi-Fi

🔧 Browser Compatibility Issues?

For best results:

  • • Use updated versions of Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
  • • Enable JavaScript in your browser
  • • Allow popups for our website
  • • Disable ad blockers temporarily
  • • Try using incognito/private browsing mode
