Data Storage and Security

Zero Storage Policy

We do not store any downloaded videos, user data, or personal information on our servers. All video processing is done in real-time and data is immediately discarded after processing.

Security Measures

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS.
  • Secure Servers: Our servers are protected by enterprise-grade security measures and regular security audits.
  • Automatic Deletion: All temporary data is automatically deleted after each session.
  • No Tracking: We do not use tracking cookies or persistent identifiers.
